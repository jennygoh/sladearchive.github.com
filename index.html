Slade school photos have been taken annually since 1931. As part of the Slade Archive Project, we are inviting alumni, scholars and members of the public to help us identify the sitters in these photographs. We have started with a small selection of photos but hope to build this collection as funds become available. If you are interested in having a particular photo digitized, or in supporting the Slade Archive Project more generally, we have launched a sponsorship scheme, further details of which can be found here. [link?]

Matching faces to names

For some photographs, we have a list of names and a corresponding indexed photograph. We need help to pair these names on our list with the faces in the photographs.

For other photographs, we do not know the names of the sitters and need help to identify them.

Our collection of class photos is also incomplete. If you know how me might obtain a copy of any of these missing photographs, please do get in touch slade.enquiries@ucl.ac.uk

Notice and Takedown Policy

If you are a rights holder and are concerned that you have found material on our website for which you have not given permission, or is not covered by a limitation or exception in national law, please contact us in writing stating the following:

  1. Your contact details.
  2. The details of the material.
  3. The url where you found the material.
  4. Proof that you are the rights holder and a statement that, under penalty of perjury, you are the rights holder or are an authorised representative.
